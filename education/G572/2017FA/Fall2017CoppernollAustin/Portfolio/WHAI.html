SCO WHAI Finder Application

My work at the State Cartographer's Office has also put me in touch with the development of the Wisconsin Historical Aerial Imagery (WHAI) Finder application. The application allows users to view and download aerial photos taken from 1937-1941 that cover the entire span of Wisconsin.

In terms of the application's development, I was tasked with creating a script that could quickly and efficiently process each photo into a JPEG thumbnail, full JPEG, and TIFF variant for the user to download.
