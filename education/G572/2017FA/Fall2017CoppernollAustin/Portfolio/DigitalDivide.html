Digital Divide Infographic

With the help of ArcMap and Adobe Illustrator I was able to construct a long-form infographic that attempted to shed light on the issue of the Digital Divide and both a global and national scale. One major component of the infographic was to provide a map that helped establish the issue in a cartographic manner, which essentially turned into an extension of my earlier work with the global connectivity map.
