SCO PLSS Application

The State Cartographer's Office's highly popular Public Land Survey System (PLSS) application has always been a core piece of its line of public use applications. PLSS points are used as the very foundation of land surveying practices in the determination of property divisions and individuals from all walks of life have found a range of meaningful uses with the app in the past.

My involvment with the app in the past had been manipulating records through spatial databases using Postgres and SQL in order to maintain the most current PLSS records from each county as they were submitted. My involvement now includes assisting the development team in migrating this current application over into a new version that includes National Geodetic Survey (NGS), United States Geological Survey (USGS), and PLSS points all in a single map with easily accessible attributes for each point. The current live version is merely an "interim" interface that is being used to hold over ongoing demand until the new version is ready to go live.
