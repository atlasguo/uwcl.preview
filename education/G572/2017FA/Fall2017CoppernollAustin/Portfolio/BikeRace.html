Haunted Midwest Bike Race Map

With this map I used a mix of Photoshop, Illustrator, and ArcMap to create a final product that would serve two purposes. The first being to offer a realistic terrain perspective of the Midwest and the second being to illustrate a fictional bike race that exhibits the element of temporal flow, with my niche interest of paranormal sites just happening to be the theme of focus.

In this case, bike racers would begin at the Summerwind Mansion in Northern Wisconsin and travel all the way down to Science Hall on the UW-Madison campus (a parnormal site itself) over the course of a week. The racers would then have a day to "free travel" to Dubuque, IA and reach Mt. Vernon, IL for a day of rest at the end of that week. On the final leg of the race, bikers would travel from Mt. Vernon to Cleveland, OH. Along the entire race, several of the cities that serve as checkpoints for the race would also contain paranormal sites that the biker would have to reach to complete that day of the race.
