Global Connectivity Map

I created this map with the intention of highlighting parts of the world that face a defecit in terms of Internet connectivity. As many other parts of the world progress on the back of technological advancement and communication through the Internet closes the communication gap between nations, some nations remain "in the dark" and face a disadvantage in terms of being able to make use of the many beneficial aspects of the Internet.
