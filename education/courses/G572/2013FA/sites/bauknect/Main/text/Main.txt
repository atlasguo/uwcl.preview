Humans have always been drawn to the mountains.  When asked why he wanted to climb Mt. Everest, mountain climber George Mallory famously responded "Because its there."  This fascination has spurred on throughout history the search for the highest mountain on Earth.  This website tells the story of that search for the highest mountain, told from a western point of view, which highlights the mountains throughout history that have been considered Earth's highest.
